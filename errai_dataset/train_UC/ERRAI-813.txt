Forge Plugin: Adds an invalid interceptor when configuring security.
When using the Errai Forge Addon to configure Errai Security it adds two interceptors to beans.xml:
{code}
<class>org.jboss.errai.security.server.SecurityUserInterceptor</class>
<class>org.jboss.errai.security.server.ServerSecurityRoleInterceptor</class>
{code}

But the {{org.jboss.errai.security.server.SecurityUserInterceptor}} class doesn't exist in Errai 3.0.3.
