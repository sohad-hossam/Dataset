GWT 2.7.0-SNAPSHOT removes some logging properties
GWT 2.7.0-SNAPSHOT removed some logging properties (gwt.logging.popupHandler and gwt.logging.firebugHandler):
https://github.com/gwtproject/gwt/commit/de218e819bd3d0b9818fe40c34999f4fa192e4fc#diff-d8f3ec69afd1e094b593ffc88e794122
