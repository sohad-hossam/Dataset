Support for wildcards and cascading when registering property change handlers
We should allow for wildcards and cascading when adding property change handlers to data binder instances.

Examples:

Wildcards: Listen for changes of any property in customer.address (e.g. customer.address.street, customer.address.postalCode)
{code}
binder.addPropertyChangeHandler("customer.address.*", handler);
{code}

Cascading: Listen for changes of any property in customer and any property of nested bindables recursively. (e.g. customer.name and customer.address.street)
{code}
binder.addPropertyChangeHandler("customer.**", handler);
{code}

Note that 
{code}
binder.addPropertyChangeHandler("*", handler)
{code}

should be equivalent to 

{code}
binder.addPropertyChangeHandler(handler)
{code}
