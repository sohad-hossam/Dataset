Allow passing of subject as parameter to MessageBuilder#createMessage() or createConversation()
Just a simple API addition. It would reduce verbosity and is pretty clear and natural. Eg.

MessageBuilder.createMessage()
                    .toSubject("HelloWorldClient")
                    .signalling()
                    .with("text", "Hi There")
                    .noErrorHandling()
                    .sendNowWith(dispatcher);

could be writter as:

MessageBuilder.createMessage("HelloWorldClient")
                    .signalling()
                    .with("text", "Hi There")
                    .noErrorHandling()
                    .sendNowWith(dispatcher);
